Philadelphia Eagles



Everyone has a different favorite football team and mine is the Philadelphia Eagles. I was drawn to that team because of the players that played there. The running back Brian Westbrook and the quarterback Donavan Mcnabb. All of the spectacular plays they did made it awesome to watch.

As time went on the Eagles were contender for the Super Bowl. They managed to get there, but could never get over the hump of losing in the super Bowl. In 2017, everything changes as the Eagles competed against the New England Patriots to conquer their first Super Bowl in franchise history.
